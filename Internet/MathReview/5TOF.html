Transformation of Figures


Translation:

(x,y)→(x+a, y+b)

Rotation:

90o counter-clockwise (270o): (x, y)→(-y, x)
180o: (x, y)→(-x, -y)
270o counter-clockwise (90o Clockwise): (x, y)→(y, -x)

Reflection:

X-axis: (x,y)→(x,-y)
Y-axis: (x,y)→(-x,y)
Y=X: (x,y)→(y,x)
Y= -X: (x,y)→(-y,-x)

Dilate by K:

(x,y)→(kx,ky)
