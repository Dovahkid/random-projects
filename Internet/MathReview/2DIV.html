Direct and Inverse Variation

Equations
Direct Variation y=kx
Inverse Variation y=k/x

Example 1

If y varies directly with x and x=7 when y=28
what is y when x=5?

28=k*7

28/7=k*7/7

4=k

y=4*5

y=20

Example 2

Same numbers but is inversely related

28=k/7

28/7=k=7*7

k=196

y=196/x

y=196/5

y=39.2
